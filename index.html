About us

The Ultimate Music Connectivity Experience

At Siren, we strive to make music connectivity as accessible as running water or electricity. We believe that humans have an inherent right to be able to compare, share, & explore each other's music—whether in the past or the current moment. The Siren Music experience allows people to visualize each other's music compatibility with their peers and significant others. For the first time, we have a tool that utilizes personalization & music data to bring your music connectivity experience to the next level.

Features

Personalize Your Profile: Customize Your Experience!

The profile is highly customizable. Allowing you to pin your favorite lyric, albums & badges. The profile allows users to explore their live & past listening history along with other features we’ll explore later.

Siren's Home Feed: Discover Music, Stories, and More!

Welcome to the Siren Home Feed; here you can explore posts made by friends & artists. Posts may include but are not limited to song recommendations, experiences, compatibility scores & DJ track lists.

Explore Profiles on Siren!

Let’s click on Nick Albury’s profile. Just like in our profile, we can see live & last listening history, posts, statistics & pinned items. Furthermore, we can delve into what makes Nick unique, like his favorite lyric & pinned albums. Across from Nick’s PFP, we’ll find a spinning green circle with a number inside. Let's explore this feature.

Musical Chemistry: Discover Your Siren Score

The Siren Score is one of our favorite features. Here, we can see our music compatibility score with Nick. Our unique Siren Score is updated as our music tastes evolve closer or further in relation to each other.

Join the Live Tune-In

The Siren Live page offers a unique, real-time exploration of your favorite artists' music, allowing you to join their session or download their song.

You might be wondering:

How does The Siren Score, live and past music history even work?

The Statistics Page is where we gather all the information to connect you to music lovers all around the globe. We don't want to keep your data from you, so we made a transparent page where you can explore it yourself. You can see your top tracks, albums, & artists this week, month, year, all time, or any custom dates you wish to explore. Use the calendar feature to go back & see what you listened to on your birthday last year.
